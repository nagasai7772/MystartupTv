Our Mission

Our mission is to empower startups and entrepreneurs by giving them a strong digital voice through engaging content, insightful storytelling, and industry exposure. We aim to be the go-to platform for showcasing innovation, sharing knowledge, and connecting ideas that drive progress.

Innovation

My Startup TV, the world’s first web TV focused only on startups. The platform shares startup stories through videos, expert talks, and event coverage, helping new businesses gain visibility. By keeping up with digital trends in content and storytelling, My Startup TV shows how media can support and grow the startup ecosystem through creativity and technology.

New restaurant venture

The team behind My Startup TV, known for their passion for innovation and storytelling, is now planning to launch a restaurant , bringing with them valuable experience in spices. With a strong understanding of startups, market trends, and flavors, this new venture reflects their entrepreneurial drive. Just as they pioneered India’s first web TV for startups, they aim to blend creativity, quality, and rich spice expertise to offer a unique dining experience.

Future Plan

The team behind My Startup TV, known for highlighting innovation and entrepreneurship, is now setting its sights on drone technology with a focus on defence applications. With a vision to contribute to national security and advanced tech development, they aim to explore how drones can enhance surveillance, logistics, and tactical operations. This forward-looking move reflects their commitment to driving innovation in critical sectors that shape the future of the nation.

Our Story

My Startup TV is a unique media platform dedicated to showcasing the vibrant world of startups and entrepreneurship. We provide insightful stories, interviews, and expert analysis, aiming to inspire and educate aspiring entrepreneurs and business enthusiasts.

Our content covers a broad spectrum, from innovative startups and emerging trends to in-depth discussions with industry leaders and investors. By offering a window into the startup ecosystem.

My Startup TV fosters a community of innovation and collaboration, empowering entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into successful ventures.
